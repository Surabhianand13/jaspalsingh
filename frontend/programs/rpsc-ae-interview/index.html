Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have cleared the RPSC AE written exam to join?

Yes, this programme is specifically for candidates who have cleared the RPSC AE 2024 written examination and are preparing for the interview/viva stage.

How is the mock interview conducted?

The mock interview on Day 3 is conducted individually in a formal board-room setting. Dr. Jaspal Singh plays the role of the board member and asks questions as a real RPSC panel would. Detailed written and verbal feedback is given after each session.

What subjects are covered in the technical review sessions?

All major Civil Engineering subjects relevant to RPSC AE are covered - Structural Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Hydraulics, Transportation, Environmental Engineering, RCC Design, and Steel Structures.

Is Rajasthan GK preparation included?

Yes. A dedicated session covers Rajasthan-specific current affairs, state government projects, PWD schemes and infrastructure developments - questions commonly asked by RPSC boards.

How do I enroll?

Click Enroll Now or WhatsApp us. Since this is a short intensive programme, seats are very limited. A brief call follows to confirm your interview date and preparation stage before confirming your seat.
