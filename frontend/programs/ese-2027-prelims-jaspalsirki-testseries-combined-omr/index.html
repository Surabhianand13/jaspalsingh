Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Home-Based OMR Test Series for ESE 2027 Prelims Paper 1 + 2?

The Home-Based OMR Test Series lets you attempt the same ESE 2027 Prelims Paper 1 and Paper 2 question papers as the offline test series - but entirely from home. You receive both question paper PDFs via email, attempt them on paper with OMR sheets, and submit the completed OMR sheets via email before end of day. Your evaluation and ranking are done together with offline participants.

What is the fee for the Combined Paper 1 + 2 Home-Based OMR Test Series?

The price is Rs 2,999 (MRP Rs 7,999). Use coupon code JASPALSIR at checkout to enroll at just Rs 1,999 - cheaper than buying Paper 1 OMR (Rs 1,499) and Paper 2 OMR (Rs 1,499) separately, which would total Rs 2,998.

What is the difference between the offline test series and this OMR test series?

The offline test series is conducted in physical centres (Jaipur, Delhi, Bhopal, Hyderabad) - students attend in person and sit in an exam hall environment. This Home-Based OMR series uses the same question papers but you attempt from home - receiving the PDFs by email and submitting your OMR sheets back by email. Rankings and evaluation are done together for both groups.

Do I need to print anything for this test series?

Yes. You will need to print both question paper PDFs (or use them on a screen as reference) and fill standard OMR sheets on paper. Then take clear photos or scans of the completed OMR sheets and submit them via email before end of day on the test date.

What happens if I miss the submission deadline?

If you do not submit your OMR sheets by end of day on the test date, evaluation will not be done for that particular test. However, you will still receive all question paper PDFs throughout the series and can use them for self-study.

When will I receive the question papers?

Question papers for both Paper 1 and Paper 2 are sent to your registered email address on each scheduled test day. The first tests are on 2 August 2026. Tests are scheduled every Sunday, matching the offline series.

How many tests are in the Combined Paper 1 + 2 OMR series?

44 tests in total - 22 for Paper 1 (10 subject-wise + 8 mixed-subject + 4 full-length) and 22 for Paper 2 (10 subject-wise + 8 mixed-subject + 4 full-length). The schedule mirrors the offline test series exactly, running from 2 August 2026 to 17 January 2027.

Is this program suitable for aspirants outside Jaipur, Delhi, Bhopal or Hyderabad?

Yes. Since it is fully home-based, aspirants from anywhere in India can enroll. The content covers the complete ESE 2027 Prelims syllabus (both papers), useful for anyone targeting this exam regardless of location.
