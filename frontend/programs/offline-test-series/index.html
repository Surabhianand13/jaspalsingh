Program Overview

The Offline Test Series is a continuous weekly testing program held every Sunday at our Jaipur centre. Questions are personally curated by Dr. Jaspal Singh and his team, covering the exact pattern, difficulty level, and topic distribution of the target exams.

Students get a full performance report after every test - subject-wise marks, rank within the batch, time analysis, and common mistake identification. A doubt-clearing session follows every test.
