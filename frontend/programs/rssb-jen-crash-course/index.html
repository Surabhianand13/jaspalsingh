What Is Included

Complete Syllabus Coverage All major Civil Engineering subjects covered in a structured sequence - theory explained concisely, followed immediately by exam-style application and practice questions.
In-Batch Mock Tests Regular mock tests scheduled throughout the crash course to track retention and flag weak areas early. Tests are reviewed in class on the same day.
Daily Doubt Sessions Dedicated time each day for questions from the previous session. No topic moves forward until all conceptual doubts from the batch are addressed.
Revision Notes and Formula Sheets Compact subject-wise revision material provided for each topic - key formulas, important concepts and high-yield exam points for quick review before the exam.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many hours per day are the classes?

Classes run for approximately 6-7 hours per day including teaching sessions and daily doubt resolution. The schedule is intensive by design to cover the full syllabus in 6 weeks.

Is this crash course for Diploma or Degree level?

This crash course covers the RSSB JEN Civil Engineering syllabus at degree level. Diploma-level aspirants are better served by the RSSB JEN Diploma Test Series.

Will study material be provided?

Yes. Subject-wise revision notes, formula sheets and practice question banks are provided throughout the course. These are prepared specifically for RSSB JEN by Dr. Jaspal Singh.

When does the next batch start?

Batch dates are announced a few weeks in advance. WhatsApp us to get notified when the next batch is confirmed and to reserve your seat early - batches fill quickly.

How do I enroll?

Click Reserve Your Seat or WhatsApp us. Seat confirmation is done after a brief call to discuss the batch date and your preparation background.
