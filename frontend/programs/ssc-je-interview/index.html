Program Overview

The SSC JE Interview Prep Workshop is a focused 3-day offline program designed specifically for candidates who have cleared the SSC JE Paper I and Paper II and are awaiting the Document Verification and Interview round.

Dr. Jaspal Singh personally conducts mock interviews, reviews candidate profiles, and provides structured feedback to help each candidate present themselves confidently and professionally.

Mock InterviewsPanel-style technical & HR mock sessions
Document VerificationGuidance on documents required & checklist
HR Round PrepCommon HR questions, how to answer confidently
Personality GroomingBody language, dressing, and communication
Written FeedbackPersonalised written report after each mock
Technical Q&ASubject-wise technical questions likely to be asked

3-Day Schedule

  • Day 1 - Technical Foundation: Review of core technical subjects relevant to SSC JE, subject-wise Q&A, identification of weak areas, and targeted preparation strategy
  • Day 2 - Mock Interview Rounds: Full panel mock interviews (technical + HR), video recording for self-review, immediate verbal feedback from Dr. Jaspal Singh
  • Day 3 - Personality & Document Prep: Personality grooming session, document verification checklist, final mock round, written feedback reports and one-on-one guidance
