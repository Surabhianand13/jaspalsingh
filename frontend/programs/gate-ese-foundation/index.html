Frequently Asked Questions

Does this course cover both GATE and ESE, or just one?

Both. The course covers the complete GATE CE syllabus and also includes the ESE-specific General Studies Paper I preparation in Phase 3. Aspirants targeting only GATE or only ESE can also benefit - the technical syllabus is largely shared between the two exams.

When should I join - how early is too early?

Joining 12-14 months before your target exam is ideal for this course. For GATE/ESE 2028 (February 2028), joining in late 2026 or early 2027 is the right window. Contact us to confirm the next batch start date.

What is the class schedule - how many days per week?

Classes are held 5-6 days a week. The exact schedule is shared before the batch begins. Doubt sessions are scheduled separately from teaching hours so neither is compromised.

Is study material provided or do I need to buy books?

Comprehensive subject-wise notes and formula sheets are provided as part of the course. Standard reference books are recommended as supplements - a reading list is shared at the start of each subject module.

How do I enroll?

Click Enroll Now or WhatsApp us. A counselling call is held to understand your background and confirm the right batch. Seats are limited to keep the batch small and effective.
