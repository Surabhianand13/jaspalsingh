FAQ

Common Questions

Where are these programs held?

All programs are held offline at our Jaipur center. The exact address is shared with confirmed participants 7 days before the program.

What is the payment process?

We use Razorpay for secure online payment — UPI, debit/credit card or net banking. A receipt is emailed automatically. Full refund if a batch is cancelled from our side.

What if I cannot attend after enrolling?

You can transfer your seat to the next available batch (within 3 months) at no extra charge. Refunds are available up to 7 days before the program start date.

Is food/accommodation included?

Lunch and tea/snacks are included for all programs. Accommodation is not included but we can suggest budget options near the venue for outstation participants.

Can I enroll in a combo of programs?

Yes! A 10% combo discount is available when enrolling in more than one program. WhatsApp us to avail it before payment.

Is there a scholarship test?

Yes — we hold periodic scholarship tests where merit-based candidates can earn up to 50% fee waiver on any batch or program. Register via the banner on the home page.
