Why This Program Is Different

The IES/ESE Interview is one of the most challenging and high-stakes interviews in India - a small panel of UPSC-appointed experts probing your technical depth, communication, and personality. Most coaching institutes guess what the panel asks. Dr. Jaspal Singh lived it - he cleared it at AIR-04.

This program is the only offline IES interview prep in Jaipur conducted by a faculty member who personally holds an AIR-04 ESE rank and has guided over 1396 candidates to ESE selection - a record recognised by the Asian Book of Records.
