Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Degree + Diploma Combo Home-Based OMR Test Series?

This combo gives you both the RSSB JE 2026 Civil Degree (28 tests) and Civil Diploma (22 tests) Home-Based OMR test series together - 50 tests in total - at a bundled price. You receive question paper PDFs via email for each category, attempt them on paper with OMR sheets, and submit before end of day. Evaluation and ranking are done together with offline participants in each category.

What is the fee for the Degree + Diploma Combo OMR Test Series?

The price is Rs 2,499 (MRP Rs 4,999). Use coupon code JASPALSIR at checkout to enroll at just Rs 1,499 - cheaper than buying the Degree OMR (Rs 999) and Diploma OMR (Rs 999) separately would normally cost at full combined price.

Do I need to choose between Degree and Diploma with this combo?

No. This combo includes both categories fully - all 28 Degree tests and all 22 Diploma tests. It is designed for candidates who want to prepare for both categories or are unsure which one they'll ultimately apply under.

What is the difference between the offline combo and this OMR combo?

The offline combo is conducted in physical centres across Rajasthan - students attend in person for both categories. This Home-Based OMR combo uses the same question papers but you attempt from home - receiving PDFs by email and submitting your OMR sheets back by email. Rankings and evaluation are done together with offline participants for both groups.

When will I receive the question papers?

Question papers are sent to your registered email address on each scheduled test day for the relevant category. Both categories start on 5 July 2026, with tests conducted every Sunday matching the offline series.

How many total tests are in this combo?

50 tests in total - 28 for Civil Degree (13 subject-wise + 15 full-length) and 22 for Civil Diploma (7 subject-wise + 15 full-length). The Degree schedule runs from 5 July 2026 to 10 January 2027; the Diploma schedule runs from 5 July 2026 to 29 November 2026.

Is this program suitable for aspirants outside Rajasthan?

Yes. Since it is fully online, aspirants from any location can enroll. The content is focused on RSSB JE 2026 Civil Degree and Diploma syllabus, useful for anyone targeting either or both categories.
