FAQ

Common Questions

Where are these programs held?

All programs are held offline at our Jaipur center. The exact address is shared with confirmed participants 7 days before the program. We are conveniently located near Jaipur Railway Station.

What's the payment process?

We use Razorpay for secure online payment. You can pay by UPI, debit/credit card, or net banking. A receipt is emailed automatically after payment. Full refund is provided if a batch gets cancelled from our side.

What if I can't attend after enrolling?

You can transfer your seat to the next available batch (within 3 months) with no extra charge. Refunds are available up to 7 days before the program start date.

Is food/accommodation included?

Lunch and tea/snacks are included for all programs. Accommodation is not included but we can suggest budget-friendly options near the venue for outstation participants.

Can I attend programs for multiple exams?

Yes! If you are preparing for both SSC JE and Rajasthan AE/JE, we recommend enrolling in both the SSC JE Interview Prep and the Rajasthan AE/JE Mock Interview. A 10% combo discount is available.
